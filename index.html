FAQ

Common Questions

Where does the data come from?

PriceCharting for per-grade trailing prices (primary), PokemonPriceTracker for eBay sold-comp medians, TCGPlayer mp-search-api for raw NM, PokemonTCG.io for card universe + Cardmarket overlay. Refreshed every 4 hours via GitHub Actions. Full methodology →

Why a quant model instead of just prices?

Price alone misses scarcity, liquidity, and momentum. One composite score lets you screen 774 cards in 10 seconds and drill in from there. See the math →

Is this financial advice?

No. Data tooling, not advice. Collectibles are speculative and illiquid. Trade your own conviction.
