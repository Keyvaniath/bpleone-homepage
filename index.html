FAQ

Common Questions

Where does the data come from?

PriceCharting for PSA 10 trailing prices, TCGPlayer for raw NM, eBay sold comps (last-5 sales), PSA pop reports. Refreshed daily via GitHub Actions.

Why a quant model instead of just prices?

Price alone misses scarcity, liquidity, and momentum. One composite score lets you screen 774 cards in 10 seconds and drill in from there.

Is this financial advice?

No. Data tooling, not advice. Collectibles are speculative and illiquid. Trade your own conviction.
