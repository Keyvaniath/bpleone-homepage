FAQ

Common Questions

Where does the data come from?

PriceCharting for PSA 10 trailing prices, TCGPlayer for raw NM, eBay sold comps (last-5 sales), PSA pop reports. Refreshed daily via GitHub Actions.

Why a quant model instead of just prices?

Price alone misses scarcity, liquidity, and momentum. One composite score lets you screen 674 cards in 10 seconds and drill in from there.

Is this financial advice?

No. Data tooling, not advice. Collectibles are speculative and illiquid. Trade your own conviction.

How do I get access after subscribing?

Email brandonpleone@gmail.com to sign up. You'll receive an access code by email; enter it in the sidebar of pokemon.bpleone.com to unlock Pro.

Can I cancel?

Yes — month-to-month, no contract. Email to cancel. Prorated refunds on Elite.

Who built this?

Brandon P. Leone — finance / equity research analyst, Southern California. Built the desk for himself + his brother, now opening it up.
