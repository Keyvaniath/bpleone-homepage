Long-form (800 chars)

Press / interview lead-in

Brandon P. Leone built bpleone.com as the front door for his personal trading operation — one operator running seven desks across collectibles, equities, options, and IB-style modeling tools. The Pokémon TCG desk is the flagship: 774 cards live-priced from PriceCharting, PokemonPriceTracker, and TCGPlayer; refreshed every 4 hours via GitHub Actions; ranked by a composite quant score (55% momentum, 20% scarcity, 15% value, 10% liquidity) with every input and threshold published on the methodology page. Sports Cards (NBA/NFL/MLB) ships the same framework. The Trading Comps Model is sold as a downloadable tool with white-label rights. The Options Pricer runs Black-Scholes Greeks with a live yfinance feed. The Equity Factor Model is a multi-factor long/short backbone. Everything's built in public.
