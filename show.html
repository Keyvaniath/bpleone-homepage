    💡 Card-show tips

    • Target buy = fair value × 0.80. 20% margin covers shipping, eBay fees, and risk.
    • Counter at target buy. Dealers expect haggling — start 10–15% below sticker.
    • Walk if the sticker is above fair value. Plenty of dealers, plenty of cards.
    • Check the card in-hand before paying. Centering, surface, edges. NM is worth 2× MP.
    • Sealed product? Open the Crack-It calc. Pokemon.bpleone.com → Crack-It Calculator.
