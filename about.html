I'm Brandon P. Leone. An investment-banking / equity-research analyst by day. By night I run a personal multi-desk trading operation across Pokémon TCG, sports cards, options, and equity factor models.

Why this exists

Most card pricing tools are either (a) raw price feeds with no opinion, or (b) social-media hype amplifiers. Neither helps an operator size a position or know when to exit.

I wanted the same rigor an IB analyst applies to a single-name equity — momentum, scarcity, value, liquidity — flattened into one number per card. Then layered transparent sanity guards so the data quality is visible, not buried.

How I work

I build in public. Every shipped feature is logged on the changelog. Every score has its math on the methodology page. The scraper, the dashboard, the data files — all in the public repo.

When the data is wrong I document the bug, the root cause, and the fix in the changelog. Recent example: the Moonbreon PSA 10 mispricing (PPT median $3,250 vs PriceCharting $4,480) — fixed by flipping the source preference for graded cards. See v0.20 changelog.

Where the data comes from

Four primary sources, ranked by trust for graded cards:

  1. PriceCharting — per-grade aggregates, 3-6mo window. Primary for graded.
  2. PokemonPriceTracker — per-grade eBay sold-comp medians, ~30d window. Fallback when PC misses.
  3. TCGPlayer mp-search-api — live market for raw NM.
  4. PokemonTCG.io — full card universe + Cardmarket EU overlay + images.

Every price overlay passes bidirectional sanity guards (lower-bound + upper-bound + grade-mapping). Failures are logged with reason codes — visible in the Price Audit tab.

What this is NOT

Not financial advice. Collectibles are speculative, illiquid, and you can lose every dollar. Trade your own conviction.

Not a recommendation engine. The quant score is one input. Pop reports, meta shifts, supply pipeline, your own balance sheet — all matter more than any single number.

Not a paid product (yet). The current beta is open access. The eventual Pro tier exists for SaaS sustainability — alerts, tax reports, position sizing — but the core quant score will stay free.
